Problem

Procurement has data, but not trusted context.

Spend, suppliers, contracts, and approvals are spread across systems, which makes sourcing slow, inconsistent, and hard to scale.

Fragmentation across ERP, AP, P-card, CLM, and supplier records
Challenge

AI alone does not solve broken operating design.

If data is messy and approvals are unclear, procurement just gets a faster version of the same confusion.

Biggest risks: weak taxonomy, poor governance, low adoption
Solution

Use agents inside a governed workflow stack.

Unify spend and supplier context first, then let agents handle event prep, supplier comparison, coordination, and follow-through.

Flow: data unification -> intelligence -> agents -> approvals
ROI

Value comes from throughput and decision quality.

Modeled impact comes from less admin work, faster sourcing cycles, more completed events, and stronger savings capture discipline.

8-25% admin reduction | 10-35% faster cycles
